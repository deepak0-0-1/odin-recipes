Tacos


Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, beans, cheese, and vegetables. They are often garnished with salsa, guacamole, sour cream, and fresh herbs.

Originating from Mexico, tacos have become a popular street food and comfort food around the world. They are known for their versatility, allowing for a wide range of fillings and toppings to suit different tastes and dietary preferences. Tacos can be enjoyed as a quick snack or a hearty meal, making them a beloved dish in many cultures.

Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 500g ground beef, chicken, or pork (or a plant-based alternative)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a Mexican blend)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salsa, guacamole, and sour cream for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Filling: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the Meat: Add the ground beef, chicken, or pork to the skillet. Cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until it is browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes. If using a plant-based alternative, cook according to package instructions.
  3. Season the Meat: Stir in the chili powder, cumin powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Warm the Tortillas: Heat a dry skillet or griddle over medium-high heat. Warm each tortilla for about 30 seconds on each side until they are pliable and slightly charred. You can also wrap them in a damp paper towel and microwave for about 20-30 seconds.
  5. Assemble the Tacos: Place a warm tortilla on a plate. Spoon a generous amount of the meat filling onto the center of the tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, and chopped cilantro.
  6. Add Toppings: Add salsa, guacamole, and sour cream as desired.
  7. Serve: Fold the tortilla in half to enclose the filling. Serve immediately while warm. Enjoy your delicious tacos!
